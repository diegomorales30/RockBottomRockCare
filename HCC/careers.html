Title: Not Quite What I Expected

I had high hopes for Rock Bottom Rock Care as a fuss-free pet solution, but it fell a bit short of my expectations. While the idea is amusing, the kit's materials could be sturdier. Setting up the habitat was simple enough, but the long-term appeal is questionable. My pet rock just sits there, and there's not much interaction or engagement. Perhaps I misunderstood the purpose, but I was hoping for something more interactive even within the realm of a pet rock. It might be great for novelty enthusiasts, but practical pet owners might find it lacking.
